    Introduction

    User stories are concise descriptions of desired features or functionality from the user's perspective. They help prioritize and deliver valuable outcomes in projects. Following a structured format, user stories focus on the user's role, their goal, and the benefit they expect. By writing user stories, we can communicate requirements, manage scope, and drive iterative development, ensuring user-centric solutions are delivered.
