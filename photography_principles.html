    Assignment

    • Research the principles on the left.
    • Take at least 5 pictures - making use of as many principles as possible and put them into your portfolio.
    • Explain for every picture which “composition rules” apply and how the use of these principles helped you come up with great pictures.

    Introduction

    Here are the most important principles of picture composition:
    • Golden Ratio
    • Lines / Angles / Distribution / Vanishing Point
    • Lines and Surfaces
    • Sharpness / DOF
    • Motion blur / Frozen Movement
    • Complementary Colors
    • Contrasts
    • Contures
    • Symmetry
    • Foreground / Middleground / Background

    I have used most of these principles for my photographs. Below are examples of how these principles can be applied in practice.

    Photos

    Conclusion

    In conclusion, I want to say that these principles are quite useful for determining the composition of the frame, but you should not dwell on them all the time. All the same, photography is an art that each person creates in his own way. You should not miss an interesting frame if it does not correspond to these principles.
