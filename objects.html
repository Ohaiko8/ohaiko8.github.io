    • Object Angles

    Assignment

    • Find three objects. That can be anything! A pen, a piece of clothing, a thing you find in the class room or something from nature.
    • Produce 5 photos with these three objects in different angles, positions and combinations.
    • Try to set a scene and think about what would look great or tells a story.
    • Put the results into your portfolio.

    Introduction

    This task is a little more useful for me than searching for faces, because I know how it can be used in practice in the future, namely in an advertisement or presentation of a product.

    Therefore, I decided to slightly change this task to suit my needs, but so that it does not deviate much from the original. I shot a cup heater using not only different angles, but also different scenes, secondary elements and effects (such as steam).

    Of course, due to insufficient lighting, I couldn’t take the highest quality photos at home (I had to increase the ISO and because of this, a little noise is visible in some photos when approaching). But in general, I am satisfied with the result and I think it turned out interesting.

    Conclusion

    In conclusion, I will say that this task was more interesting and useful for me than looking for faces, although it is an alternative one. My opinion is that it should go as mandatory, since as a designer it seems to me that in the future I will have to take a lot of product photos or be able to evaluate such photos, which is no less important than being able to take them.
