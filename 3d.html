    • 3D in After Effects

    Goal

    Create a 3d scene in After Effects using plug-in Element 3D to learn how to use it and practice before working on 3d for group video project.

    Introduction

    I've just tried to implement my vision of idea for group project in this small video. I spent 2 whole days working in After Effects, Element 3D, Premiere Pro, Blender, Photoshop and other programs to achive this result. I do think that it could be done better in terms of lightning, texture detalization, but at the same time I realy think that it's a nice video for the first attempt. I also had to use a blanket instead of green screen at home, so person on background can be not that realistic because I just cuted a mask of him buy pen tool. Note: I used some stock videos and sounds in the beginning so it can look cringy because of lack of sync audio with video.
