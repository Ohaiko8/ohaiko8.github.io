    Introduction

    In today's digital age, three-dimensional (3D) models have revolutionized the way we perceive and interact with virtual environments. With their immersive capabilities and realistic representations, 3D models have found applications in various fields, ranging from gaming and architecture to medical simulations and product design. Harnessing the power of 3D models, I started exploring the potential of my innovative extension idea.

    Conclusion

    In conclusion, I centered around testing the extension idea of incorporating 3D models into a web shop has shed light on the tremendous potential of this integration. By leveraging the power of 3D visualization, I aimed to enhance the web shopping experience and provide customers with a more immersive and interactive way to engage with products. Through comprehensive testing and evaluation, I have gained valuable insights into the feasibility, advantages, and challenges associated with adding 3D models to web shops.

    The results of testing highlight the transformative impact of 3D models on customer engagement, product visualization, and purchase decision-making. By allowing customers to rotate, zoom, and interact with products in a virtual environment, 3D models provide a more realistic representation that bridges the gap between physical and online shopping.
