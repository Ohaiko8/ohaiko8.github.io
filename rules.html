    • Group Rules

    Assignment

    Create rules for the team that you will adhere to in the future.

    Introduction

    One of the first group assignments was to develop rules to help maintain discipline during project development. In our rules, everything is based on a small punishment in the form of buying drinks for the rest of the participants for not fulfilling their duties. Although, as it turned out in the end, this does not prevent some participants from ignoring the entire work process.

    Document

    Conclusion

    In conclusion, I want to say that despite the agreed rules, some of the project participants still ignore all the work. My opinion is that those who are motivated to work will still try to work on the project, even if it is hard. And those who did nothing are unlikely to change due to several rules described in the document.

    Therefore, as for me, these rules should either be completely removed because of their uselessness, or they should be tightened. Suppose if a person does not get in touch for more than a week without a good reason, then he is given a warning, and as a result, exclusion from the group for several such misconduct.
