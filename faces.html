    • Hidden Faces

    Assignment

    • Find and photograph many hidden faces - select at least three of them as your best ones.

    • Put the results into your Portfolio to show off your findings.

    Introduction

    Finding hidden faces was quite difficult. I had ideas from the very beginning to take pictures of cars and buildings, but it was very obvious. And I wanted to find something more interesting, but it did not work out very well.

    I really spent a lot of time on this task, but I managed to find only a couple of interesting examples. I didn’t take pictures of the cars because I’m sure that 60% of the students will have at least 1 car and tried to do something unusual.

    Due to the fact that this task did not work out perfectly for me, I also decided to spend time to an alternative with objects from different angles.

    Photos

    Conclusion

    In conclusion, finding hidden faces in photography is a fun and engaging activity. It forces you to pay attention to all the little things that you see and try to show them through photography.

    What really surprised me was that although the task seemed easy at the beginning, it turned out to be quite difficult.
