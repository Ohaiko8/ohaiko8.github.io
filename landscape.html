    • Landscape

    Assignment

    • Take many landscape / architecture photos and explore how settings and focal length influence the pictures and proportions.

    • Select your three absolute best photos and put them into your Portfolio.

    • Add a description that outlines why you chose settings and focal length for your top three.

    • Bonus: Edit your landscape / architecture photos with Photoshop, add the original and edited picture on an extra (double) page of your portfolio.

    Introduction

    I love taking landscape photos. And I did them on the camera of my phone long before this task, so I had an idea of what I want to see as a result. The hardest part was finding interesting places in Eindhoven. Due to the fact that at the moment I live and study in the Netherlands, where nature is far from being as beautiful and diverse as in other countries, I decided to wait for my trip to Spain on vacation and take some beautiful shots.

    Regarding camera setting I usually used Av, because I needed to set the rigth aperture and iso parameters. I tried to not use a wide angle lens, because I'm not a big fan of messed up proportions. And also I saw no need in using it (besides skyscrapers photo).

    Conclusion

    In conclusion, landscape photography is a captivating art form that allows us to capture the beauty of nature. Through careful composition, lighting techniques, and patience, stunning images are created. Ultimately, it is a personal journey of self-expression that offers a fulfilling and enriching experience.
