This task was not very difficult to create, but very difficult to understand. The fact is that this task, like many others, does not have a description. To be honest, I didn’t spend a lot of time on it, maybe halfway through I realized that this was not what I needed. I wanted to make the animation a lot more varied, but using SVG is either very difficult or impossible to do. Therefore, as for me, it's easier to just animate the desired element in Adobe After Effects, which I plan to do with my logo and other elements.
