    Assignment

    Perform card sorting on your group project!

    Introduction

    In this assignment, my main goal was to test my extension ideas in terms of UX and UI design. I also really wanted to get more experience of communicating with strangers, as in one of the interviews in the first part. In this assignment, I will focus on moderated in person usability testing, and I also want to try remotely-moderated usability testing.

    Conclusion

    In conclusion, the testing extension idea of adding 3D models on a web shop in terms of UX and UI testing has proven to be a valuable addition to enhance the overall user experience and improve user interface design.

    Firstly, the ability to zoom in, rotate, and view products from different angles enhances the sense of product exploration and engages users on a deeper level, increasing their overall satisfaction with the shopping experience.

    Secondly, UI testing has indicated that integrating 3D models into a web shop can improve the visual appeal and intuitiveness of the user interface. The seamless integration of 3D models with the existing design elements creates a cohesive and immersive experience. Proper placement and sizing of the models within the UI prevent clutter and ensure a smooth browsing experience.

    Moreover, the testing of the 3D models extension has also revealed some potential areas for improvement. Providing clear instructions or tutorials on how to navigate and interact with the 3D models can be beneficial for users who may be less familiar with this technology.

    Overall, the testing extension of adding 3D models on a web shop has demonstrated its potential to positively impact both the UX and UI aspects of the shopping experience.
