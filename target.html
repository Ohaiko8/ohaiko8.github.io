    Assignment

    Pick a company do research into the target group for that company.

    • Who is the target group?
    • Age, gender, education etc. (if relevant to the company)
    • What are the wishes and needs of that target group?
    • Apply at least 2 research methods and describe why.
    • Create a persona based on research.
    • What is the company doing and not doing correct based on their users needs?
    • Prepare a 10 minute presentation for next week.
    • What and why you used your research method.
